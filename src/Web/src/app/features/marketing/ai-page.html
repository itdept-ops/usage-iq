shield

Multimodal, and private by posture.

The two camera features — Snap a meal and Scan label — send your photo to Google Gemini to read the food; it is not stored by Usage IQ, and a one-time notice tells you so before the first use. Images are downscaled in your browser first. Every AI feature is gated by the tracker.ai permission, off by default — when it is off, the assists simply disappear and the tracker still works.
