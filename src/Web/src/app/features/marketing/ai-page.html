shield

Multimodal, and private by posture.

The two camera agents — Snap a meal and Scan label — send your photo to Google Gemini to read the food; it is not stored by Usage IQ, and a one-time notice tells you so before the first use. Images are downscaled in your browser first. The whole agent layer is gated by the tracker.ai permission, off by default — when it is off, the agents simply disappear and the OS still runs.
